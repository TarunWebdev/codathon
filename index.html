CODATHON

WHAT IS CODATHON?

NCU CODATHON is a university-wide competetion to provide students a platform to solve some real-life problems we face in our daily lives, while simultaneously developing skills for product innovation and their mindset of problem-solving.
The position holders will get cash prizes and the winner will also get a chance to showcase their project. Participantion certificates will be provided to all.

  

DURATION

This hackathon will be a 24 hour competetion to be held on the

20th Feb 2021 (00:00) - 21st Feb 2021 (00:00)

This coding event which, considering the pandemic situation, will be organised virtually.The event time will split into various rounds and break time.

ABOUT US..

CODE ARTS is an SIG (STUDENT INTREST GROUP) of NCU Student Branch dediated to creating a community of not only proficient coders,but also of individuals skilled in domains like data mining, pattern recognition, machine learning, image processing ,business analytics etc. We aim to equip each of our members with outsanding coding skills, starting right from the basics. From writing rudimentary programs to developing advanced websites and applications, the initiatives of this SIG will help make this transition smooth for aspiring coders. In the past,we have organised several successful events virtually, which more than one thousand people attended. We have invited speakers from eminent organisations like Google, Amazon etc to deliver talks.
