CODATHON

ABOUT CODATHON

NCU CODATHON is a university-wide competetion to provide students a platform to solve some real-life problems we face in our daily lives, while simultaneously developing skills for product innovation and their mindset of problem-solving.
The position holders will get cash prizes and the winner will also get a chance to showcase their project. Participantion certificates will be provided to all.

DURATION

This hackathon will be a 24 hour competetive coding event which, considering the padnemic situation, it will be organised virtually.

The event time will split into various rounds and break time.

  OF ROUNDS

    2. Advanced data structure
    DEVELOPMENT

    3. system software Requirement
    4. Final project

ABOUT US..

CODE ARTS is an SIG (STUDENT INTREST GROUP) of ncu student branch dediated to creating community of not only proficient coders,but also of individuals skilled in domains like data mining, pattern recognition, machine learning and image processing and business analytics, etc. We aim to equip each of our members with oustanding coding skills, starting right from basics. From writing redimentory programs to developing advances websites and applications, the initiatives og this SIG will help make this transition smooth for aspiring coder. In the past,we have organised several successfull events virtually, where more than 1K people attended. We have invited speakers from eminent organisations like Google, Amazon etc to dilever talks.
